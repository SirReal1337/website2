Sustainable Plastics Home Improvements is one of Lancashires most popular and approved installers of double-glazing windows, doors and conservatories. We have a big selection of styles, colours and finishes to suit your home.

Windows

Keep your home warm, quiet and safe with new windows from Sustainable Plastics. Our uPVC windows, wooden windows and aluminium windows come in a variety of styles and colours to suit your home. Save on your heating bills with durable, A-rated double glazing as standard with long guarantees.

What are the benefits of our double-glazed windows? Replacing your single-glazed windows with double-glazed windows is an upgrade worth the spend. There are many benefits to installing double-glazed windows.

They include retaining heat, reducing noise pollution and improving the value of your home. Our double-glazed windows are fitted with an advanced locking system to keep your property safe and secure. It’s time to release your inner designer and give your home a fresh lease of life.

uPVC Windows

If you're looking for a classic, low-maintenance window that won't fade or rot, you'll find it with our uPVC design. Durable and secure, our uPVC windows are available across our whole range of styles, and can be customised in a wide selection of colours, including woodgrain finishes.

Each uPVC window is made to order using the latest technology and our own unique formula, designed to match your home and keep it more secure and energy efficient. They're what make us an industry leader, and uPVC double glazing our most popular choice.

Aluminium Windows

The clean looks and light, modern material used in our double glazed aluminium windows lends a contemporary style to properties of any age. They're high performing and energy efficient, made from strong, polyester powder-coated frames for a long-lasting finish that won’t corrode.

We can also configure the windows in any way you choose, to best suit your home. With lots of colours – including dual colours – and a selection of decorative glass, fixtures and glazing options available, Our double glazed aluminium windows are truly bespoke.

Wooden Windows

Add character and the beauty of natural wood to your home with wooden double glazed windows. They are especially suited to listed buildings or properties in a conservation area.

Choose from sustainably-sourced soft or hardwood windows. Wooden windows are crafted combining age-old joinery skills with modern technology. They are fitted with secure multi-point locking systems available in a range of finishes and a choice of colours.
